FAQ
What counts as a "project"? +
A project is one URL analyzed — DemoAgent crawls the site, detects user flows, records clips, and generates a stitched video. You can re-run a project if your product updates without it counting as a new one within the same billing period.
How long does a project take to generate? +
Most projects complete in 4–8 minutes from URL submission to downloadable MP4. Studio plan projects with priority encoding typically finish in under 3 minutes. Complex sites with many flows may take slightly longer.
Can it record sites that require login? +
Yes. You can provide credentials securely and the agent will authenticate before recording. Credentials are used only for that session and never stored. This is available on all paid plans.
What happens if I go over my project limit? +
You can purchase additional project credits at $14 each, or upgrade your plan mid-cycle. We'll never auto-charge you — you stay in control.
Do you offer refunds? +
If a project fails to generate due to a technical issue on our end, we credit your account immediately. Subscriptions are cancellable any time; we don't offer partial-month refunds but you keep access until the end of the billing period.
